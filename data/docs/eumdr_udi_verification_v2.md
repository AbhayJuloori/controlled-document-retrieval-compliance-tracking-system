# SOP UDI Label Verification

## Purpose
Add a second-person check for multilingual label content.

## Controlled Requirements
1. Two qualified reviewers shall review the final label proof before packaging release.
2. A barcode verification grade of B or better is acceptable for release.
3. Translation discrepancies shall be escalated within 4 hours of detection and before the affected lot is packaged.
