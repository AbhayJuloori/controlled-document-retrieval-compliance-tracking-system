# Policy Electronic Records and Signatures

## Purpose
Move to risk-based access controls with tighter account disablement.

## Controlled Requirements
1. Privileged account passwords shall be rotated every 60 days.
2. Electronic signature exception logs shall be reviewed every two weeks by IT Quality and System Administration.
3. Inactive administrator accounts shall be disabled within 8 hours of role change.
