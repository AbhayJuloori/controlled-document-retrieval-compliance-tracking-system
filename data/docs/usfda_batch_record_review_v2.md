# SOP Batch Record Review

## Purpose
Tighten review timing following an inspection observation.

## Controlled Requirements
1. Batch production records shall be reviewed within 1 business day after manufacturing completion.
2. The reviewer shall escalate any critical deviation to the site quality head immediately and no later than 4 hours from discovery.
3. A quality assurance reviewer, the manufacturing supervisor, and the release authorizer shall sign the batch record disposition.
