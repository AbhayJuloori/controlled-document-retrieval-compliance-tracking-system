# Policy Supplier Qualification and Monitoring

## Purpose
Define baseline supplier qualification expectations.

## Controlled Requirements
1. High-risk suppliers shall receive an on-site audit at least once every 12 months.
2. A quarterly supplier performance score below 80 shall trigger escalation to the Supplier Review Board.
3. Quality and Procurement directors shall approve the addition of a critical supplier.
