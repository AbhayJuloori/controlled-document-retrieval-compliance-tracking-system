# Policy Supplier Qualification and Monitoring

## Purpose
Reduce surveillance interval for high-risk suppliers.

## Controlled Requirements
1. High-risk suppliers shall receive an on-site audit at least once every 9 months.
2. A quarterly supplier performance score below 85 shall trigger escalation to the Supplier Review Board.
3. Quality, Procurement, and Regulatory directors shall approve the addition of a critical supplier.
