# CAPA Summary Internal Audit Escalation

## Purpose
Tighten closure timing for recurring system findings.

## Controlled Requirements
1. Internal audit CAPA items shall be closed within 45 calendar days unless an extension is approved.
2. More than three overdue internal audit CAPA items shall trigger executive review.
3. The Internal Audit manager and the Head of Quality shall approve CAPA deadline extensions.
