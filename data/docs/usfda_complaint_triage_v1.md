# SOP Complaint Triage and MDR Assessment

## Purpose
Standardize initial complaint triage and MDR screening.

## Controlled Requirements
1. Initial complaint triage shall be completed within 5 calendar days of complaint receipt.
2. Missing complainant information shall be requested within 3 business days of case opening.
3. Potential MDR events shall be escalated to Regulatory Affairs within 2 business days.
