# SOP Batch Record Review

## Purpose
Control the review of batch production records before lot release.

## Controlled Requirements
1. Batch production records shall be reviewed within 2 business days after manufacturing completion.
2. The reviewer shall escalate any critical deviation to the site quality head within 1 business day.
3. A quality assurance reviewer and the manufacturing supervisor shall sign the batch record disposition.
