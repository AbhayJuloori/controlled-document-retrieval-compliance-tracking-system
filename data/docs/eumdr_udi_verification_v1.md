# SOP UDI Label Verification

## Purpose
Control label verification prior to packaging release.

## Controlled Requirements
1. One labeling specialist shall review the final label proof before packaging release.
2. A barcode verification grade of C or better is acceptable for release.
3. Translation discrepancies shall be escalated before the affected lot is packaged.
