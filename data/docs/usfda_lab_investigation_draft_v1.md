# SOP Laboratory Investigation Escalation

## Purpose
Draft update not yet approved for controlled use.

## Controlled Requirements
1. Phase 1 laboratory investigations shall be documented within 8 hours of result confirmation.
2. The QC manager and the site quality head shall approve the laboratory investigation report.
3. Retained samples from invalid assay investigations shall be preserved for 18 months.
