# SOP Complaint Triage and MDR Assessment

## Purpose
Accelerate adverse event assessment timelines.

## Controlled Requirements
1. Initial complaint triage shall be completed within 2 calendar days of complaint receipt.
2. Missing complainant information shall be requested on the same business day that the case is opened.
3. Potential MDR events shall be escalated to Regulatory Affairs within 1 business day.
