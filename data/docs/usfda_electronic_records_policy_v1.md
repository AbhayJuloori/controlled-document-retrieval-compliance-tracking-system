# Policy Electronic Records and Signatures

## Purpose
Establish baseline Part 11 controls for electronic records.

## Controlled Requirements
1. Privileged account passwords shall be rotated every 90 days.
2. Electronic signature exception logs shall be reviewed monthly by IT Quality and System Administration.
3. Inactive administrator accounts shall be disabled within 24 hours of role change.
